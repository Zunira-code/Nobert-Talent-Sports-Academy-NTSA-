About NTSA

Nobert Talent & Sports Academy (NTSA) was founded to bridge the gap between raw talent and professional excellence. Based in Kenya, we serve as a lighthouse for aspiring volleyball athletes, providing them with the mentorship and resources needed to reach the highest levels of the sport.

Our Mission

To empower youth through sports scholarships and world-class technical training.

Message from the CEO
